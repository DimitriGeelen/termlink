channel claim` so an operator can reserve a unit of work on a topic
in one keystroke instead of remembering argument order. Mirror of
T-2093 `/claims` (READ-side) — completes the substrate #1 daily-verb
surface across both directions.

T-2092..T-2095, every successful invocation produces a real claim
envelope on the hub with a lease. Treat like a write. Surface
refusal modes loudly (CLAIM_ALREADY_HELD, AUTH_FAIL, RATE_LIMITED)
with actionable next-step hints rather than silent retry.
